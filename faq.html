FAQ

Questions before you apply.

Here are the basics. If you are unsure whether coaching fits your goal, send an application and I will review it.

Do I need gym experience?

No. Your program will match your current experience level, and we can start with the basics if you are new.

Do I need a gym membership?

No. Gym and home training options are available depending on your equipment and goals.

How often do we check in?

Weekly. Check-ins help us track progress, make adjustments, and keep you accountable.

Can I lose fat and build muscle?

Yes, especially if you are newer to structured training. The exact approach depends on your starting point.

Will I get a meal plan?

You will get nutrition guidance and targets. The goal is to build habits you can actually follow.

What happens after I apply?

I review your application and follow up with the next step if coaching is a good fit.

Still deciding?

Send your goals and I will take a look.
